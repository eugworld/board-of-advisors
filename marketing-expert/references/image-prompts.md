# AI Image Generation Prompt Library for Marketing

A comprehensive collection of prompts for generating professional marketing visuals using Midjourney, DALL-E, Ideogram, or Flux. All prompts follow a clean, minimal aesthetic with the brand color #2ABFAB (teal/mint green).

**Best Tool:** Midjourney, Ideogram
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Prompt:**

## Style Guidelines & Tips

### Color Palette
- **Primary Brand Color:** #2ABFAB (mint green/teal)
- **Complementary Colors:** Dark Navy (#1a1a1a), Warm Gray (#666666)
- **Backgrounds:** White (#ffffff), Soft Gray (#f9f9f9)
- **Accents:** Can vary based on mood

### Design Principles
- **Minimalism:** Clean, uncluttered compositions
- **Professional:** Business-appropriate even when creative
- **Modern:** Current design trends but not trendy/temporary
- **Scalable:** Works across different sizes and contexts
- **Readable:** Leaves room for text overlays

### Prompt Writing Tips
1. **Be Specific:** Include aspect ratio and technical details
2. **Use Style Keywords:** "minimalist", "clean", "geometric", "abstract"
3. **Avoid Generic:** Don't ask for "professional looking" without details
4. **Specify Color:** Always mention #2ABFAB or "mint green"
5. **Set Mood:** Include emotional descriptors when relevant
6. **Think Context:** Consider where the image will be used

### Tools Comparison
- **Midjourney:** Best for complex compositions, high quality, artistic control
- **DALL-E:** Good for specific objects, realistic imagery, quick iterations
- **Ideogram:** Excels at text in images, bold designs, graphic style
- **Flux:** Fast, diverse styles, good for variations and iterations

### Quality Enhancement
Add these phrases to improve results:
- "High resolution"
- "Professional lighting"
- "Clean composition"
- "No artifacts"
- "Polished finish"
- "Studio quality"
